# Agriculture & Food Management: The 2026 Landscape

> [!ABSTRACT] Executive Summary
> Indian agriculture is undergoing a structural shift. While crop production remains vital, the **Allied Sectors** (Livestock, Fisheries) are now the primary growth engines. The narrative has shifted from "Food Security" to **"Nutritional Security and Economic Resilience."**
>
> **Key Stat:** Agriculture accounts for **1/5th of National Income** but employs **46.1%** of the workforce.
