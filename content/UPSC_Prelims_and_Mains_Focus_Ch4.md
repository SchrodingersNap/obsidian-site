## üîç Prelims Facts / Keywords
* **Economic Complexity Index (ECI):** India ranks **44th**. Measure of knowledge/sophistication in export basket.
* **Complexity Outlook Index (COI):** India ranks **2nd**. Measures *potential* to diversify.
* **Top Remittance Source:** **USA** (surpassed UAE/Gulf).
* **Top Service Export:** Software (48%), followed by Business Services.
* **Net International Investment Position (NIIP):** India is a **Net Debtor**.
* **Fully Accessible Route (FAR):** Allows non-residents to invest in specific G-Secs without limits.
* **Voluntary Retention Route (VRR):** For FPIs in debt markets (committing to hold for 3 years).
