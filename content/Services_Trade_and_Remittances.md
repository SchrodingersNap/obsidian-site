## 1. Services Trade: The Stabilizer
* **Surplus:** **$188.8 Billion** (FY25).
* **Impact:** This surplus covers **two-thirds** of the merchandise trade deficit.
* **Structural Shift:**
    * **Software:** Remains dominant (48% share).
    * **Business Services:** Rising fast due to **Global Capability Centres (GCCs)**.
* **AI Advantage:** India ranks **2nd globally** in AI skill penetration (Stanford AI Index).

## 2. Remittances: The Unsung Hero
* **Inflows:** **$135.4 Billion** (FY25 Provisional).
* **Comparison:** Remittances > Gross FDI Inflows.
* **Source Shift:**
    * *Old:* Gulf/GCC dominated (Low-skilled).
    * *New:* **Advanced Economies** dominate (High-skilled).
    * **Top Source:** USA (27.7%), followed by UAE, UK, Singapore.
