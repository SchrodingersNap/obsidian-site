*An innovative proposal in the Survey:*
Using procurement savings to fund diversification.
* **Current State:** FCI holds excess buffer stocks of Rice/Wheat -> High carrying cost.
* **Proposal:** Use the money saved from *not* storing excess grain to incentivise farmers in Punjab/Haryana to switch to **Pulses and Oilseeds**.
* **Benefit:** Reduces import bill, improves soil health (legumes fix nitrogen), and solves the storage glut.
