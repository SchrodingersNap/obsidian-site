## 1. Inflation Dynamics
* **Headline CPI:** Dropped to **1.7%** (Apr-Dec FY26).
* **Drivers:** Correction in Vegetable/Pulses prices + Base Effect.
* **Core Inflation:** Persistent due to *Gold/Silver* prices. If excluded, core is soft.
* **Policy:** Monetary easing (125 bps Repo rate cut since Feb 2025).

## 2. Fiscal Policy
* **Strategy:** Consolidation + Capex.
* **Target:** **4.4% Fiscal Deficit** by FY26 end.
* **Quality of Spend:**
    * Capex share in total exp: 12.5% (FY20) $\rightarrow$ **22.6%** (FY25).
    * [[SASCI]]: Centre incentivizing States to maintain capex (2.4% of GDP).
* **Result:** Sovereign Rating Upgrades (S&P to BBB, CareEdge to BBB+).

## 3. External Sector (The Challenge)
* **Merchandise Trade:** Exports grew 2.4%; Imports 5.9%.
* **Services Trade:** Strong surplus counters goods deficit.
* **Balance of Payments (BoP):**
    * **H1 FY26:** **Deficit** of $6.4 Billion (vs Surplus last year).
    * *Reason:* **FPI Outflows** (Shift to US AI stocks) + Trade deficit.
* **Rupee:** Depreciated ~6.5% (Orderly movement).
* **Buffers:** Forex Reserves cover >11 months of imports.
