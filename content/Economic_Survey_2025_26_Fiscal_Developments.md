> [!ABSTRACT] Executive Summary
> In a global environment of elevated public debt, India stands out for its "Credible Consolidation." 
> * **Fiscal Deficit (Centre):** Budgeted at **4.4%** for FY26 (Down from 9.2% in FY21).
> * **Strategy:** Revenue buoyancy (Tax Reforms) + Expenditure Rebalancing (Revenue $\rightarrow$ Capital).
> * **Outcome:** Sovereign Rating Upgrades (S&P, Morningstar, R&I).
> * **New Anchor:** Debt-to-GDP target of **50 ¬± 1%** by FY31.
