India's growth (7.4%) is internally driven.

## 1. Demand Side (Expenditure)
* **[[Private Final Consumption Expenditure]] (PFCE):**
    * Share in GDP: **61.5%** (Highest since FY12).
    * Growth: 7.5%.
    * *Driver:* Rural demand recovery (NABARD survey shows 79% households reported increased consumption).
* **[[Gross Fixed Capital Formation]] (GFCF - Investment):**
    * Share in GDP: **30%**.
    * Growth: 7.6%.
    * *Driver:* Sustained Public Capex + Revival in Private Corporate Investment.
